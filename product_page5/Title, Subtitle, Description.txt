Gate & Simulation
	Design & share circuits online

Description 
Designed with the student in mind! Create quick and simple logic gate diagrams for classes, or use it to teach beginners the basics! Logic Gate Editor is a user-friendly circuit design tool created for the beginning student. It takes only the core functionality of popular the circuit editor Logisim, eliminating the complexity that can be daunting to first-timers.
Share your work! Logic Gate Editor allows you to easily save and share created diagrams, as well as load other user’s work to your browser!
